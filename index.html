Design Knowledge

Types of Logos

Every brand is unique. I create all 7 types of professional logos — tell me your vision and I'll bring it to life.

01

Pictorial Mark

An image or icon that directly represents the brand in a simple visual form.

eg: Apple, Shell, WWF
02

Lettermark

Initial letters forming a clean, compact brand identity.

eg: HBO, H&M, IBM
03

Wordmark

Brand name styled with unique custom typography that stands on its own.

eg: Coca-Cola, Disney, Google
04

Combination Mark

Text and symbol combined into one powerful, versatile logo.

eg: Puma, Amazon, Adidas
05

Emblem

Text enclosed inside a symbol, badge or seal for a traditional feel.

eg: Harley-Davidson, Starbucks
06

Abstract Logo

Geometric shapes with a deeper, hidden meaning unique to your brand.

eg: Nike, Pepsi, Mastercard
07

Mascot Logo

A fun character or illustration that becomes the face of your brand.

eg: KFC, Pringles, Michelin
